Online Presence

Rate Your Online Presence
Grade each question from 1 (not at all) to 5 (absolutely)
01 — What is your Google rating right now?
Below 4.0 is a red flag for most buyers. Between 4.2 and 4.7 is the sweet spot — a perfect 5.0 with very few reviews is actually trusted less.
No profile or below 4.0 4.2 – 4.7 stars
02 — How many Google reviews do you have?
Volume builds trust. Businesses with 50+ reviews are far more likely to show up when people search locally. Under 10 and most buyers won't take you seriously.
Under 10 reviews 50+ reviews
03 — Do you reply to your reviews — especially the bad ones?
Replying to reviews, including negative ones, shows future customers that you care. Businesses that respond regularly earn significantly more than those that don't.
Never Every single one
04 — When someone visits your website, is it immediately obvious what you do and how to get in touch or buy?
If a visitor has to think for more than a few seconds, they leave. One clear message and one clear button is all it takes — most websites have neither.
No website or very confusing Crystal clear, easy to act
05 — Are you posting regularly on the platform where your customers actually are?
Instagram and Facebook for lifestyle and local businesses. LinkedIn for B2B. TikTok for younger audiences. Being active on the wrong platform is the same as not being active at all.
Wrong platform or rarely post Right platform, posting consistently
06 — Do people comment, share or message you directly because of something you posted?
Likes don't pay the bills. Comments, shares and direct messages are the signs that your content is actually connecting with potential customers.
Almost never Regularly
07 — Are you listed and active on the main platform people in your industry use to find and compare businesses?
Restaurants and hotels: TripAdvisor. Tours: GetYourGuide. Beauty: Treatwell. Healthcare: Doctolib. Home services: Houzz. If your buyers search there, you need to be there and look better than the competition.
Not listed or badly maintained Complete profile, great reviews
