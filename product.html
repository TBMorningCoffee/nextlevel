Product Market Fit

The strongest businesses don't just sell a product — they sell a specific outcome to a specific person in a way nobody else does. A private chef doesn't sell recipes. A great marketing agency doesn't sell views. They sell results. This section measures how clearly your product is positioned and how strongly the market feels it.

Grade each question from 1 (not at all) to 5 (absolutely)
